Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 13, 2026

KitchenProof respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your data from our platform — including any data collected through our WhatsApp Business API integration and Google Sign-In.

This page also serves as the Data Deletion Callback URL as required by Meta's platform policies for apps using the WhatsApp Business API and Facebook Login.

What Data We Hold

Depending on how you use KitchenProof, we may hold the following data about you:

  • Your name, email address, and Google profile information (from Google Sign-In).
  • Your WhatsApp phone number (if you are a registered team member in an organization).
  • Task completion records and compliance history associated with your account.
  • Photo proof images you submitted via WhatsApp or the web app.
  • WhatsApp message logs (inbound and outbound) associated with your phone number.
  • Login session data and activity logs.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data in the following ways:

Option 1 — Email Request (All Users)

Send an email to privacy@kitchenproof.in with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".

Please include in your email:

  • Your full name.
  • The email address associated with your KitchenProof account (or the phone number if you are a WhatsApp-only user).
  • The name of the organization you were part of (if known).
  • Whether you want to delete your individual data only, or close and delete the entire organization account.

We will confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete the deletion within 30 days.

Option 2 — In-App Account Deletion (Organization Owners)

If you are the Owner of an organization on KitchenProof:

  1. Sign in to KitchenProof at your app URL.
  2. Navigate to Settings.
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone section.
  4. Click "Delete Organization" and confirm.

This will permanently delete your organization, all users, all task data, and all associated records within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

Upon a confirmed deletion request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account profile (name, email, Google ID).
  • Your WhatsApp phone number and message history associated with our system.
  • All task completion records and compliance history linked to your account.
  • All photo proof images you submitted.
  • Any organization data if you are the sole owner.

Some anonymized, aggregated statistical data may be retained for internal analytics and cannot be linked back to you.

We may retain certain data for longer if required by applicable law or for legitimate legal purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).

Confirmation of Deletion

After completing the deletion, we will send a confirmation email to the address you provided in your request, confirming that your data has been deleted from our systems.

Facebook / Meta Data

If you connected your account via a Meta product or your data was processed through the WhatsApp Business API, you can also manage your Facebook data directly through Facebook's tools:

  • Facebook's Data Deletion Tool
  • Off-Facebook Activity

Please note that deleting data from Facebook does not automatically delete data from KitchenProof's systems. You must submit a separate request to us using the methods above.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

KitchenProof ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our platform — including our web application and WhatsApp-based services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Name, email address, and Google account details when you sign in via Google OAuth.
  • Team member information: Names, email addresses, roles, and WhatsApp phone numbers of staff added to your organization by an account owner or manager.
  • Task and operational data: Task templates, task completion records, compliance notes, and any text submitted through the platform.
  • Photo proof: Images uploaded by staff as proof of task completion, submitted via WhatsApp or the web application.
  • Organization details: Kitchen name, address, and timezone configured in the platform settings.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, timestamps, and interaction logs within the web application.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.
  • WhatsApp messages: Inbound messages sent to our WhatsApp Business number by staff members (used solely for task completion processing).

1.3 Information from third parties

  • Google: Basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) obtained via Google Sign-In with your consent.
  • Meta / WhatsApp: Message content and phone number metadata from the WhatsApp Business API, used to power task completion and notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the KitchenProof platform.
  • Create and manage user accounts and organization workspaces.
  • Send WhatsApp notifications, task reminders, and welcome messages to staff members.
  • Process task completions received via WhatsApp messages and attach photo proof to compliance records.
  • Generate compliance reports, analytics, and dashboard data for organization owners and managers.
  • Respond to support inquiries and communications.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. WhatsApp Data Usage

KitchenProof uses the Meta WhatsApp Business API to send and receive messages on behalf of registered organizations. Specifically:

  • We send automated task reminder messages to staff phone numbers registered in the platform by organization administrators.
  • We send welcome messages to newly added team members.
  • We receive and process inbound WhatsApp messages to allow staff to mark tasks as complete and submit photo proof.
  • WhatsApp messages and media are processed to update task records and are stored securely in our database.
  • Phone numbers are stored only for the purpose of sending operational notifications and are not used for marketing or shared with third parties.

Our use of WhatsApp data complies with the WhatsApp Business Policy and the Meta Platform Terms.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Third-party vendors who assist in operating our platform (e.g., cloud storage providers for proof images, database hosting). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Meta / WhatsApp: As required to operate the WhatsApp Business API integration.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not share your data with any other third parties for commercial purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained while your organization account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account closure upon request.
  • Task and compliance records: Retained for up to 2 years to support audit requirements, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Photo proof images: Retained for up to 1 year, then deleted from our storage.
  • WhatsApp message logs: Retained for 90 days for operational purposes, then deleted.

You may request deletion of your data at any time. See Section 7 (Your Rights) and our Data Deletion page.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption.
  • Passwords and authentication tokens are never stored in plain text.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Photo proof images are stored in secure cloud storage with access controls.
  • We perform regular security reviews of our platform and dependencies.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data. See our Data Deletion page for instructions.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@kitchenproof.in.

8. Children's Privacy

KitchenProof is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.

9. Cookies

Our web application uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Maintain authentication sessions (HTTP-only cookies for security).
  • Remember your language and theme preferences.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform.

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect certain platform functionality.

10. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
